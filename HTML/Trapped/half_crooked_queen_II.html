The Half Crooked Queen Ⅱ combines the moves of the Girl Scout and the Bishop. That is, it zigzags orthogonally outward, or slides diagonally outwards.

Game Piece Info
Half Crooked Queen Ⅱ

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Half Crooked Queen Ⅱ can move like the Wazir, so it follows the spiral and visits all the squares.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Half Crooked Queen Ⅱ can move like the Blind Monkey, so it follows the spiral and visits all the squares.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Half Crooked Queen Ⅱ can move like the Flying Cock, so it follows the wedge and visits all the squares.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Half Crooked Queen Ⅱ visits all the squares by visiting all the squares by following a simple pattern. The pattern is different from the pattern followed by the Crooked Queen and the Half Crooked Queen Ⅰ.
