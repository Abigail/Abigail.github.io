The %%PIECE%% moves like a Nightrider, except that it alternates between two different Knight moves on each step. It will always move outwards, and every second move is either on diagonally three squares away, or orthogonally four squares away.

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% does not get trapped in the first 10,000 steps. It's unlikely it will later on.

Flat

The %%PIECE%% does get trapped on the Flat Wedge, after no more than 21 steps.
