%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% combines the moves of the Crooked Rook and the Crooked Bishop. That is, it zigzags orthogonally or diagonally outward.

Game Piece Info
Crooked Queen

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the %%PIECE%% can move like the Wazir, so it follows the spiral and visits all the squares.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the %%PIECE%% can move like the Blind Monkey, so it follows the spiral and visits all the squares.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% can move like the Flying Cock, so it follows the wedge and visits all the squares.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% visits all the squares by visiting all the squares of each row, before moving on to the next row.
