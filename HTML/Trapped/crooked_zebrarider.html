%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves like a Zebrarider, except that it alternates between two different Zebra moves on each step. It will always move outwards, and every second move is either on diagonally five squares away, or orthogonally six squares away.

Game Piece Info
Crooked Zebrarider

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the %%PIECE%% does not get trapped in the first 10,000 steps. The Zebrarider gets trapped after 266 steps, and the Zebra gets trapped after 4,633 steps.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the %%PIECE%% does not get trapped in the first 10,000 steps. The Zebrarider gets trapped after 1,613 steps, and the Zebra gets trapped after 14,469 steps.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped after 358 steps, which is more than the Zebrarider, which gets trapped after 72 steps. The Zebra gets trapped after 80 steps.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped after 122 steps, which is more than the Zebrarider, which gets trapped after 57 steps. The Zebra gets trapped after 286 steps.
