Violent Bear

by Abigail
. . . . . . . . L . . . L . . . * . * . . . . * S * . . . . * . * . . . . . . . . . Shogi: 1

The Violent Bear either slides up to two unoccupied squares diagonally forward (like a very limited forward Bishop), or steps one square diagonally backward (like a backward Ferz), or one square orthogonally sideward (like a sideward Wazir).

Name Game(s) Info
Violent Bear Taikyoku Shōgi This is one of the version of the Violent Bear which exist. See Violent Bear for the other one.

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Violent Bear gets trapped rather quickly: after 93 steps.

Diamond

On the Diamond Square, the Violent Bear moves like the Blind Monkey, visiting all the squares in order.

Wedge

Folded

The Violent Bear can move like the Flying Cock, so it visits all the squares of the Folded Wedge in order.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Violent Bear visits the all the squares using the same path the King uses.
