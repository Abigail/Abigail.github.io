Actor

by Abigail

The Actor is a piece from Overkill Ecumenical Chess.

The Actor combines the movements of the Bishop, and the Gnu. (The Gnu combines the movements of the Knight and Camel.)

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Actor does not get trapped in its first 20,000 steps. The pattern it follows is at that point still a bit irregular, so it's unclear whether it will get trapped or not.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Actor fills the entire Wedge, using an interesting pattern. It fills the Wedge in a balanced way.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the entire board is filled as well, using a different interesting pattern. The Actor has a slight bias to the right hand side of the board.
