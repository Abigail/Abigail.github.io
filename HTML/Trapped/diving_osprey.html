The Diving Osprey either moves up to four unoccupied squares in any orthogonal direction (like the limited Rook), two squares in each of the four diagonal directions (like the Alfil), or one square in one of the two diagonal forward directions (like the forward Ferz).

Square

The Diving Osprey can move as the Wazir, so it will just follow the Spiral.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Diving Osprey uses a complicated pattern to fill the entire board.

Wedge

Folded

The Diving Osprey can move as the Flying Cock so it will just follow the Folded Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Diving Osprey fills the entire board following a slightly interesting pattern, with a bias to the right hand side of the board.
