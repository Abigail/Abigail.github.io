The Cardinal either slides an unlimited number of unoccupied squares diagonally (like a Bishop), or jumps one or three squares orthogonally (like a Wazir or Threeleaper).

Name Game(s) Info
Cardinal Ganymede Chess ☋Is promoted Bishop

Spiral

Square

% ./trapped -m 5k -l s_sq —div r FFHW % Box: [-1, 1] [-1, 1] +------+------+------+ | . | 1225 | . | +------+------+------+ | 1260 | * | 1255 | +------+------+------+ | . | 1260 | . | +------+------+------+

The Cardinal can move as a Wazir. This means that Cardinal will follow the Spiral, visiting all the squares in order.

As can be seen from the heatmap to the right, the Cardinal makes about the same number of steps in each of the four orthogonal directions.

Diamond

% ./trapped -m 5k -l s_d —div l FWW % Box: [-1, 1] [-1, 1] +------+------+------+ | 1275 | . | 1225 | +------+------+------+ | . | * | 50 | +------+------+------+ | 1225 | . | 1225 | +------+------+------+

The Cardinal can move as the Blind Monkey. Hence, it will follow the spiral and visit all the squares on the board.

The Cardinal will make mostly diagonal moves, roughly the same in each of the four diagonal directions, and a single step to the right for each "layer" of the spiral.

Wedge

Folded

% ./trapped -m 5k -l w_fo —div r FFHW % Box: [-1, 0] [-1, 1] +------+------+------+ | 35 | . | 35 | +------+------+------+ | 2480 | * | 2450 | +------+------+------+

Since the Cardinal can move as the Flying Cock, it will do so, filling the entire board visiting all squares in value order.

The Cardinal will mostly move sideways, and make one step diagonally forward when reaching the edge of the board.

Flat

% ./trapped -m 10k -l w_fl —div l -z 5 FFHW % Box: [-1, 0] [-3, 3] +------+------+------+------+------+------+------+ | . | . | . | . | 100 | . | . | +------+------+------+------+------+------+------+ | 3267 | . | 1716 | * | 1716 | . | 3201 | +------+------+------+------+------+------+------+

The Cardinal uses a simple pattern, visiting all the squares in a row before moving to the next one.

As can be seen from the heatmap on the left, most of the moves the Cardinal makes are one and three square orthogonally sideways moves, with single square diagonally moves to go the next "row" of the layout.
