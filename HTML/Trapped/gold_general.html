%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either moves one step in an orthogonal direction (like a Wazir), or one step diagonally forward, like a forward Ferz.

Spiral

Since the %%PIECE%% can move like the Wazir, it does so, filling the board by following the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

Since the %%PIECE%% can move like the Flying Cock, it does so, filling the board by following the Wedge.

Flat

The %%PIECE%% fills the Flat Wedge going from right to left, and left to right, making a small sidestep just before reaching the left edge. Compare this to the King and Dragon Horse, which do something similar, with with a slightly different sidestep.
