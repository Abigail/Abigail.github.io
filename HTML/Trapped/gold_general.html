Gold General

by Abigail

The Gold General (金將) either moves one step in an orthogonal direction (like a Wazir), or one step diagonally forward.

The Gold General is a piece in Shōgi (将棋). It appears in the starting array, but several pieces also promote to the Gold General: the Silver General (成銀), the Lance (成香), the Knight (成桂) and the Pawn (と金). The promoted Pawn is often called a Tokin.

In Typhoon, the Gold General promotes to the Horned Owl.

In Dōbutsu shōgi, the Chick promotes to the Hen, which moves a the Gold General.

Spiral

Since the Gold General can move like the Wazir, it does so, filling the board by following the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

Since the Gold General can move like the Flying Cock, it does so, filling the board by following the Wedge.

Flat

The Gold General fills the Flat Wedge going from right to left, and left to right, making a small sidestep just before reaching the left edge. Compare this to the King and Dragon Horse, which do something similar, with with a slightly different sidestep.
