Gold General

by Abigail

The Gold General (金將) from Shogi (将棋) either moves one step in an orthogonal direction (like a Wazir), or one step diagonally forward.

Shogi has a few promoted pieces which move like a Gold General: the Promoted Silver General (成銀), the Promoted Katsura Horse (成桂) the Promoted Incense Chariot (成香) (aka Promoted Lance), and the Reaches Gold (と金) (aka Promoted Foot Soldier aka Promoted Pawn).

Since the Gold General can move like a Wazir, it does so, filling the board by following the spiral.
