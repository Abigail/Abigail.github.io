by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves as either the Wazir, the Dabbaba or the Alfil. This means, the %%PIECE%% jumps two squares away diagonally or one or two squares orthogonally,

Spiral

Since the %%PIECE%% can move as the Wazir, it will follow the Spiral, visiting all squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% almost follows the path of the Wedge, except for making swapping two squares at each new row. It still visits each square on the board.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, for each row, the %%PIECE%% first visits all the even squares (going left to right), then all the odd squares (going right to left). It then moves the the next row.
