Champion

by Abigail

The Champion moves as either the Wazir, the Dabbaba or the Alfil. The piece is found in Omega Chess.

In Carrera's Chess, dating from the early 17th century, there was also a piece named Champion, but this was a Rook + Knight compound.

Since the Champion can move as the Wazir, it will follow the Spiral, visiting all squares on the board.

On the Wedge, it almost follows the path of the Wedge, except for making swapping two squares at each new row. It still visits each square on the board.
