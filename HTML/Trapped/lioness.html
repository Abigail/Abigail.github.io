%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% combines the movments of the Wazir, Ferz, Dabbaba, Knight and Alfil. This means, the %%PIECE%% can jump anywhere in a 5 x 5 square centered around the starting position (but it can't jump to the starting square).

Diamond

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Flying Cock, so it will follow the Folded Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the fills the board row by row, where each row has half its squares visited on a left to right sweep, then the rest is visited on a right to left sweep. When all the squares in a row have been visited, the %%PIECE%% moves to the next row.
