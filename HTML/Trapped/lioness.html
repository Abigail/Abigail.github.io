Lioness

by Abigail

The Lioness combines the movments of the King, Dabbaba, Knight and Alfil. This means, the Lioness can jump anywhere in a 5 x 5 square centered around the starting position (but it can't jump to the starting square).

In Scirocco, the Goat promotes to the Lioness.

Spiral

The Lioness can move as the Wazir, so it will follow the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

The Lioness can move as the Flying Cock, so it will follow the Folded Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the fills the board row by row, where each row has half its squares visited on a left to right sweep, then the rest is visited on a right to left sweep. When all the squares in a row have been visited, the Lioness moves to the next row.
