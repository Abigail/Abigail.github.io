%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves as a blocking Zebra. That is, the %%PIECE%% moves one square orthogonally, and then two squares diagonally outward — and those squares it moves over must be unoccupied. (Unlike the Zebra which can leap to the same squares as the %%PIECE%% can, but which cannot be blocked).

Square

On the Square Spiral, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped quickly, after no more than 55 steps.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spirla, the %%PIECE%% remains free for only slightly longer: it gets trapped after 60 steps.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped quickly as well, after 47 steps.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped even faster: 18 steps.
