%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves to the same squares as the Knight moves to, but it cannot jump. It either moves one square orthogonally, then one square diagonally outward, or one square diagonally, then one square orthogonally outward. The first square may not be occupied.

This combines the movement of the Mao and the Moa.

Game Piece Info
Quantum Chess Bowman ⎋This piece has additional capture moves,
which we can ignore here

Link: Cⱽ.

Spiral

Square

The %%PIECE%% gets trapped after 78 steps, which is a little bit more than the Mao or Moa, but a lot less than the Knight.

Diamond

The %%PIECE%% gets trapped faster than on the Square Spiral: after 55 steps, it is trapped.

Wedge

Folded

Only 10 steps, and the %%PIECE%% is trapped.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% moves in the same way as on the Folded Wedge, and hence gets trapped after 10 steps.
