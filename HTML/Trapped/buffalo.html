%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either leaps to a square which is one away in one orthogonal direction and two the other (like a Knight), leaps to a square which is one away in one orthogonal direction, and three in the other (like a Camel), or to a square which is two away in one orthogonal direction, and three in the other (like a Zebra).

This makes the %%PIECE%% a Compound Leaper.
