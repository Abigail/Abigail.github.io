%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either moves an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares orthogonally forward or backward (like a vertical Rook), or an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares diagonally forward (like a forward Bishop).

The movements of the %%PIECE%% is the mirror image of the movements of the Whale.

Game Piece Info
Chu Shōgi (中将棋) White Horse 白駒 ☋Is promoted Lance

Spiral

The %%PIECE%% will visit almost all squares in a wedge to the right of the origin. As such, in the limit, the %%PIECE%% will visit 25% of the squares on the board.

This mirrors the squares visited by the Whale.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% escapes to the upper right, by visiting the three right most squares of each row.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% escapes by clinging to the right edge of the Wedge.
