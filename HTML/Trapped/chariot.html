Chariot

by Abigail

The Chariot is a short rook: a piece which moves like a Rook, but with a limited range. The Chariot slides up to four unoccupied squares in an orthogonal direction.

The Chariot is a piece in Scirocco, and it promotes to the Octopus.

Spiral

On the Spiral, it can move as the Wazir, hence it fills the board by following the spiral.

Wedge

Folded

Just like the Rook, the Chariot gets trapped after six steps.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Chariot escapes. It visits the two right most squares of each row; the travel pattern for the Chariot is the same as for the Rook.
