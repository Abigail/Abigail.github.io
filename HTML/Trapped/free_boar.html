%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares diagonally (like the Bishop), or orthogonally leftward or rightward (like the sidewards Rook).

Compare with the Flying Ox which has the same movements, rotated 90°.

Square

Despite the Bishop and the sidewards Rook not getting trapped, nor the Queen (which combines the Bishop and full Rook), the %%PIECE%% does get trapped, after 251 steps. This is less than half the steps the Flying Ox takes before getting trapped.

Diamond

THe %%PIECE%% can move as the Blind Monkey, so it visits all the squares by just following the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded Wedge

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Flying Cock, hence the %%PIECE%% follows the Folded Wedge, visiting all the squares on the board.

Flat Wedge

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% follows a simple pattern, visiting every square on the board, with a strong bias to the right.
