Free Boar

by Abigail

The Free Boar moves an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares diagonally (like the Bishop, or orthogonally leftward or rightward (like the sidewards Rook).

Compare with the Flying Ox which has the same movements, rotated 90°.

In Typhoon, the Free Boar is the promoted Ghost Warrior.

Spiral

Despite the Bishop and the sidewards Rook not getting trapped, nor the Queen (which combines the Bishop and full Rook), the Free Boar does get trapped, after 251 steps. This is less than half the steps the Flying Ox takes before getting trapped.

Wedge

Folded Wedge

The Free Boar can move as the Flying Cock, hence the Free Boar follows the Folded Wedge, visiting all the squares on the board.

Flat Wedge

On the Flat Wedge, the Free Boar follows a simple pattern, visiting every square on the board, with a strong bias to the right.
