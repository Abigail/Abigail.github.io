The Counsellor either moves as the Rook, Bishop, or the Bowman. That is, it moves an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares in each of the eight main directions, or it jumps like a Knight, provided it does not jump over an occupied square.

which we can ignore here

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Counsellor can move as the Wazir, and hence it follows the spiral.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Counsellor can move as the Blind Monkey, and hence, it follows the spiral.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Counsellor can move as the Flying Cock, and hence, it follows the spiral.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Counsellor visits all the squares on the board, following a simple pattern. It will make diagonally forward Ferz moves, sideways Wazir moves, and both Moa and Mao moves.
