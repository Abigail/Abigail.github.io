%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares diagonally (like the Bishop, or orthogonally forward or backward (like the forward/backward Rook).

Compare with the Free Boar which has the same movements, rotated 90°.

Spiral

Despite the Bishop and the forward/backward Rook not getting trapped, nor the Queen (which combines the Bishop and full Rook), the %%PIECE%% does get trapped, after 540 steps.

Folded Wedge

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% escapes, visiting the three right most squares on each row.

Flat Wedge

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% escapes, visiting the right most square on each row.
