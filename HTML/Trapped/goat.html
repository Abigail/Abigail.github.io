Goat

by Abigail

The Goat either jumps to a square one away diagonally (like the Ferz), or two away orthogonally (like the Dabbaba). This makes the Goat a colourbound piece.

In Scirocco, the Goat promotes to the Lioness.

Spiral

The Goat follows the spiral, but it visits only every second square.

Wedge

Folded

THe Goat follows the Folded Wedge, visiting every second square.

Flat

The Goat fills half the board, row by row, visiting every second square. Before reaching the left side of the board, a small side step is taken.
