Wazir

by Abigail

The Wazir is one of the basic leapers. It's a very old piece, appearing in very early chess variants. It may step one square in each of the four orthogonal directions.

As such, it can always follow the spiral. It will not get trapped, and it will eventually visit every square on the board. This is true for any piece whose possible moves are a superset of the moves of a Wazir. This includes the King, Queen and Rook from traditional chess.
