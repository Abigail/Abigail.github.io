%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves like either the Camel or the Giraffe. This makes the %%PIECE%% a Compound Leaper, jumping to a square which is one away in one orthogonal direction, and three or four way in the other.

The %%PIECE%% does not get trapped in its first billion steps. But it doesn't become clear whether it starts following a pattern or whether it remains free.

Diamond

The %%PIECE%% bounces around wildly, only to get trapped after 46,359 steps.

Wedge

Neither on the Folded nor on the Flat Wedge does the %%PIECE%% get trapped for its first billion steps. But it remains unclear whether it never does.
