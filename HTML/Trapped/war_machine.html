The War Machine moves like either the Wazir or the Dabbaba. That is, it jumps either one or two squares in an orthogonal direction. It may jump over an occupied square.

Other names for the War Machine include: Machine, and Wazaba.

Spiral

Square

Since the War Machine can move as the Wazir, it will just follow the Spiral, filling the entire board.

Diamond

The War Machine will visit all the squares using an interesting pattern.

Wedge

Folded

The War Machine fills the Folded Wedge row by row, going left to right on even rows, and right to left on odd rows.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the War Machine fills the board row by row as well, but each row requires two passes: a left to right sweep visiting the even squares, and a right to left sweep visiting the odd squares.
