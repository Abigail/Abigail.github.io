Zebra 🦓

by Abigail

The Zebra is one of the basic leapers, a (2, 3)-leaper. This means that the Zebra jumps to a square which is two squares away in an orthogonal direction, and 3 squares away into a direction perpendicural to the first direction.

The Zebra will circle in an irregular way around the origin, with some focus of activity on the diagonals.

The Zebra will get trapped after 4633 moves.
