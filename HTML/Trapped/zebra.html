Zebra 🦓

by Abigail

The Zebra is one of the basic leapers, a (3, 2)-leaper. This means that the Zebra jumps to a square which is three squares away in an orthogonal direction, and two squares away into a direction perpendicular to the first direction.

In Scirocco, the Guard promotes to the Zebra.

Spiral

The Zebra will circle in an irregular way around the origin, with some focus of activity on the diagonals. The Zebra will get trapped after 4,633 steps.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Zebra bounces around for 80 steps, then it gets trapped.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Zebra has a strong bias to the right hand side of the board. It gets trapped after 286 steps.
