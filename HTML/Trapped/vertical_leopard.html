Vertical Leopard

by Abigail
. . A . . . . * . . . . * . . . * * * . . * S * . . . * . . . . . . . Shogi: 1

The Vertical Leopard either steps one square diagonally forward (like a forward Ferz), steps one square orthogonally backward or sideways (like a horizontal/backward Wazir), or slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares orthogonally forward (like a forward Rook).

Name Game(s) Info
Vertical Leopard Taikyoku Shōgi

Spiral

Square

On the Folded Wedge the Vertical Leopard moves like the Wazir, so it visits all the squares on the board.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Vertical Leopard uses a two step loop to move one square orthogonally forward, giving it an escape velocity of \(\frac{1}{2}\).

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge the Vertical Leopard moves like the Flying Cock, so it visits all the squares on the board.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Vertical Leopard uses a simple pattern to visit all the squares.
