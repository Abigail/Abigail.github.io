Otter

by Abigail

The Otter either moves one square diagonally (as a Ferz), or one square orthogonally forward (as a forward Wazir), or up to four unoccupied squares sideways (as a limited sideways Rook).

In Typhoon, the Otter promotes to the Missionary.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Otter gets trapped after 321 steps.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Otter can move as the Flying Cock, and hence, will follow the Folded Wedge, visiting all the squares.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Otter visits all the squares by using a pattern biased to the right hand side.
