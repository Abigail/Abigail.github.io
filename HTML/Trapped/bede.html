%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either leaps to a square which is two away in any of the four orthogonal directions (like a Dabbaba), or slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares in any of the four diagonal directions (like a Bishop). The %%PIECE%% is colourboundA colourbound piece will always stay on a square of the same colour: if it starts on a black square, it will always remain on a black square; if it starts on a white square, it will always remain on a white square.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the %%PIECE%% visits every second number of the Spiral; hence, the %%PIECE%% will visit 50% of the squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% visits every second number of the Wedge; hence, the %%PIECE%% will visit 50% of the squares on the board.

Flat

The %%PIECE%% will use a simple pattern to visit 50% of the squares on the board.
