The %%PIECE%% either moves one square forward (either orthogonally or diagonally), or one square orthogonally backward.

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% visits the three right most squares of each row, escaping to infinity that way.

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% visits the right most square of the each row.
