Copper General

by Abigail

The Copper General either moves one square forward (either orthogonally or diagonally), or one square orthogonally backward.

In Typhoon, the Copper General promotes to the Ibis.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Copper General circles the origin once, then escapes to bottom right.

Wedge

Folded Wedge

On the Folded Wedge, the Copper General visits the three right most squares of each row, escaping to infinity that way.

Flat Wedge

On the Flat Wedge, the Copper General visits the right most square of the each row.
