Genie

by Abigail

The Genie moves as the Queen, but only up to three squares. That is, the Genie can slide up to three unoccupied squares in any orthogonal or diagonal direction.

In Scirocco, the Genie is the promoted Dabbaba. In Typhoon, the Genie is the promoted Salamander.

The Harpy in Scirocco and Typhoon (to which the Dervish promotes) moves in the same way as the Genie. In addition, the Harpy gives some addtional movement powers to friendly which are in reach, but we can ignore that in our setup. The Harpy cannot capture.

The Typhoon, as found in Typhoon moves in the same way as the Genie. In addition, it may capture any enemy piece (so, up to three) it finds in its path (but we ignore captures). The Cicada promotes to the Typhoon.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Genie can move as the Wizar, so it will fill the board by following the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Genie can move as the Flying Cock, so it will fill the board by following the Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Genie fills the board by following a slightly slanted pattern.
