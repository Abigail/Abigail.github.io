On each move, the Zebra Rider makes one or more steps as a Zebra, provided it doesn't turn, and each step lands on an unoccupied square. The Zebra is a (3,2)-Leaper, that is, each move it moves to a square three away in one orthogonal direction, and two away in the other orthogonal direction.

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Zebra Rider gets trapped after 266 moves, which is a lot quicker than the Zebra, which doesn't get trapped until step 4,633.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Zebra Rider gets trapped after 1,613 steps, which is a lot sooner than the Zebra, which takes 14,569 steps before it gets trapped.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Zebra Rider gets trapped after 72 steps; compare the Zebra, which gets trapped on move 80.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Zebra Rider gets trapped after 57 steps. This is a lot sooner than the Zebra, which doesn't get trapped until move 286.
