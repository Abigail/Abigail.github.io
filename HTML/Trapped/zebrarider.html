Zebrarider

by Abigail

On each move, the Zebrarider makes one or more steps as a Zebra, provided it doesn't turn, and each step lands on an unoccupied square. The Zebra is a (3,2)-leaper, that is, each move it moves to a square three away in one orthogonal direction, and two away in the other orthogonal direction.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Zebrarider gets trapped after 266 moves, which is a lot quicker than the Zebra, which doesn't get trapped until step 4633.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Zebrarider gets trapped after 72 steps; compare the Zebra, which gets trapped on move 80.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Zebrarider gets trapped after 57 steps. This is a lot sooner than the Zebra, which doesn't get trapped until move 286.
