The Beaver either moves one square orthogonally or diagonally forward (like a forward Wazir or a forward Ferz), up to four unoccupied squares orthogonally sideways, or an unlimited of unoccupied squares orthogonally backward (like a backward Rook).

Game Piece Info
Typhoon Beaver ☊Promotes to Sorcerer

Spiral

Square

Since the Beaver can move as the Wazir, the Beaver will follow the Spiral, visiting every square on the board.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Beaver escapes upward, using a two step loop to move on square upward. This means an escape velocity of \(\frac{1}{2}\).

Wedge

Folded

Since the Beaver can move as the Flying Cock, the Beaver will follow the Folded Wedge, visiting every square on the board.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Beaver uses a fairy unique and simple pattern to to visit all the squares on the board.
