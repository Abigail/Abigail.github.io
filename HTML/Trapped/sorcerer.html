Sorceror

by Abigail
. . . . . . . . L . L . L . . . * * * . . . L * S * L . . . * * * . . . L . L . L . . . . . . . .

The Sorcerer moves up to two unoccupied squares in each of the eight main directions. It is a very limited Queen.

Compare with the Squire which can move to the same set of squares, but which cannot be blocked.
