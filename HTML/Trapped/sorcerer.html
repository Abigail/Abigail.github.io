The Sorcerer moves up to two unoccupied squares in each of the eight main directions. It is a very limited Queen.

Spiral

Square

The Sorcerer can move as the Wazir, and as such, if will follow the Spiral.

Diamond

The Sorcerer can move as the Blind Monkey, and as such, if will follow the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

The Sorcerer can move as the Flying Cock, and as such, if will follow the Folded Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Sorcerer will visit all the squares.
