King

by Abigail

The Chess King (♔) can step one square away in all eight directions. Shogi (将棋) has Kings as well, the King General (王將) and Jeweled General (玉將), which moves in exactly the same way. (King General and Jeweled General are names for the same piece, but of opposite sides).

A Mann is a piece which moves in exactly the same way, but a Mann is not royal. (Royal pieces are pieces which need lead to a loss of the game when they are captured). Royalness however, does not matter in our investigation. A Prince is another name for a Mann.

In the Shogi variant Dōbutsu shōgi, the King is known as the Lion.

Spiral

The King can move as the Wazir, and the Wazir will follow the Spiral. Hence, the King will visit all the squares on the board.

Wedge

The King can move as the Gold General, and the Gold General will follow the Wedge. Hence, the King will visit all the squares on the board.
