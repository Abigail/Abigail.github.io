King

by Abigail

The King (♔) can step one square away in all eight directions. Shōgi (将棋) has Kings as well, the King General (王將) and the Jeweled General (玉將), which moves in exactly the same way. (King General and Jeweled General are names for the same piece, but of opposite sides).

A Mann is a piece which moves in exactly the same way, but a Mann is not royal. (Royal pieces are pieces which need lead to a loss of the game when they are captured). Royalness however, does not matter in our investigation. A Prince is another name for a Mann.

In the Shōgi variant Dōbutsu shōgi, the King is known as the Lion.

Spiral

The King can move as the Wazir, and the Wazir will follow the Spiral. Hence, the King will visit all the squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

The King can move as the Flying Cock, and the Flying Cock will follow the Folded Wedge. Hence, the King will visit all the squares on the board.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the King fills the board row by row, alternating going with and against the numbers, with a small twist just before reaching the left hand side of the Wedge.
