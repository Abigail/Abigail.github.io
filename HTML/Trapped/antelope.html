The Antelope is a (3,4)-leaper. That is, the Antelope jumps to a square which is three away in one orthogonal direction, and four in the other.

Game Piece Info
Basic Leapers Antelope

Link: Cⱽ

Spiral

Square

The Antelope gets trapped after 1,887 steps on the Square Spiral

Diamond

The Antelope takes much longer to get trapped on the Diamond Spiral, than it does on the Square Spiral: it gets trapped after 101,249 steps.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Antelope gets trapped relatively fast; it gets trapped after 128 steps.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Antelope survives a bit longer than on the Folded Wedge, but it still gets trapped. After 416 steps, it's trapped.
