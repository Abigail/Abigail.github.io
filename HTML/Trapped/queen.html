Queen ♕

by Abigail

The Queen can slide an unlimited number of squares in all eight directions: four orthogonal directions and four diagonal directions. It cannot slide over occupied squares.

Spiral

The Queen can move like a Wazir, and a Wazir can follow the Spiral. Hence, the Queen will fill the entire board, and not get trapped.

Wedge

Folded

The Queen can move like the Gold General, and hence will follow the Wedge, filling the entire board.

Flat

The Queen will fill the entire board, but in doing so, it has a strong bias on the right hand side of the board.
