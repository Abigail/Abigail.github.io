%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% can slide an unlimited number of squares in all eight directions: four orthogonal directions and four diagonal directions. It cannot slide over occupied squares. The %%PIECE%% combines the movements of the Rook and Bishop.

It's common in many Chess variant, using different names:

Spiral

The %%PIECE%% can move like a Wazir, and a Wazir can follow the Spiral. Hence, the %%PIECE%% will fill the entire board, and not get trapped.

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% can move like the Flying Cock, and hence will follow the Wedge, filling the entire board.

Flat

The %%PIECE%% will fill the entire board, but in doing so, it has a strong bias on the right hand side of the board.
