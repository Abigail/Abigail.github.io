The Queen can slide an unlimited number of squares in all eight directions: four orthogonal directions and four diagonal directions. It cannot slide over occupied squares. The Queen combines the movements of the Rook and Bishop.

It's common in many Chess variant, using different names:

Spiral

Square

The Queen can move like a Wazir, and a Wazir can follow the Spiral. Hence, the Queen will fill the entire board, and not get trapped.

Diamond

The Queen can move like a Blind Monkey, and a Blind Monkey can follow the Spiral. Hence, the Queen will fill the entire board, and not get trapped.

Wedge

Folded

The Queen can move like the Flying Cock, and hence will follow the Wedge, filling the entire board.

Flat

The Queen will fill the entire board, but in doing so, it has a strong bias on the right hand side of the board.
