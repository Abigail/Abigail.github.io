Queen ♕

by Abigail

The Queen can slide an unlimited number of squares in all eight directions: four orthogonal directions and four diagonal directions. It cannot slide over occupied squares. The Queen combines the movements of the Rook and Bishop.

In Scirocco, and Typhoon, the Queen is the promoted Stork.

The Vulture in Scirocco moves like the Queen (but it cannot capture on its diagonal moves which are not direction adjacent to its starting square). The Vulture is the promoted Scirocco.

The Immobilizer moves (but does not capture) as the Queen. It will prevent any adjacent piece (from the other side) to move. In Typhoon, the Immobilizer is the promoted Hummingbird.

The Longleaper moves (but not captures) like the Queen. It has additional hopping capture moves, but we ignore those. In Typhoon, the Cuckoo promotes to the Longleaper.

Spiral

The Queen can move like a Wazir, and a Wazir can follow the Spiral. Hence, the Queen will fill the entire board, and not get trapped.

Wedge

Folded

The Queen can move like the Flying Cock, and hence will follow the Wedge, filling the entire board.

Flat

The Queen will fill the entire board, but in doing so, it has a strong bias on the right hand side of the board.
