The Okapi moves like either the Knight or the Zebra. That is, the Okapi jumps to a square which is two away in one orthogonal direction, and one or three away in the other.

Game Piece Info
Compound Leapers Okapi

Spiral

Square

It takes almost a million steps, but the Okapi gets trapped after 947,668 steps.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, it's not clear whether the Okapi gets trapped or not. It does not get trapped for its first billion steps.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, it's not clear whether the Okapi gets trapped or not. It does not get trapped for its first billion steps.

Flat

After almost 300 steps, the Okapi enters a four step loop which brings it one square forward and to the right. This gives it an escape velocity of \(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4}\).
