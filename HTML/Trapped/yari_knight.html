Yari Knight

. . A . . . . * . . . * * * . . . * . . . . S . . . . . . . Shogi: 1

The Yari Knight moves as either the Shōgi Knight, or the Lance (a forward Rook). That is, the Yari Knight either jumps to a square which is two squares away forward, and one sideways, or it slides an unlimited number of squares forward.

Game Piece Info
Yari Shōgi Yari Knight ☊Promotes to Yari Gold

Spiral

On either Spiral, the Yari Knight escapes in a straight line forward, by moving one square forward each step. The escape velocity is \(1\).

Wedge

On both the Folded and Flat Wedge, the Yari Knight escapes in a straight line forward. The escape velocity is \(1\).
