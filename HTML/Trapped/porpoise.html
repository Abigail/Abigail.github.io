%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves one square either to the left or right. This means, the Porpoise will always stay on the same row.

Game Piece Info
Whale Shōgi (鯨将棋) Porpoise On capture, the Porpoise turn into the Killer Whale, which can be dropped by the capturing player.

Spiral

On either Spiral, the %%PIECE%% escapes to the right, with an escape velocity of \(1\), by stepping right on each step.

Wedge

The %%PIECE%% cannot move on either Wedge.
