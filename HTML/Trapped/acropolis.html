Acropolis

by Abigail

The Acropolis is a piece from Overkill Ecumenical Chess.

The Acropolis combines the movements of the Rook, and the Gnu. (The Gnu combines the movements of the Knight and Camel.)

Spiral

Since the Acropolis can move as the Rook, and hence, as the Wazir, the Acropolis fills the entire board by just following the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

The Acropolis fills the entire Folded Wedge, in an interesting pattern, going from one side to another, bouncing back and forth between three rows at a time.

Flat

The Acropolis also fills the entire Flat Wedge, using a different, but also interesting, pattern. It has a bias the right hand side of the Wedge.
