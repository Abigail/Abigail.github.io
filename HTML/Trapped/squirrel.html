Squirrel

by Abigail

The Squirrel moves either like the Dabbaba, the Knight or the Alfil. That is, the Squirrel jumps to a square which is two away in one orthogonal direction, and no more than two in the other.

In Scirocco, the Camel promotes to the Squirrel.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Squirrel doesn't get trapped in the first 20,000 steps. It may get trapped later, or remain free.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Squirrel does not get trapped, and it visit every square of the Wedge in an interesting pattern.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Squirrel doesn't get trapped in the first 20,000 steps. It may get trapped later, or remain free.
