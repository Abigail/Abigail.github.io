Blind Monkey (盲猿)

by Abigail

The Blind Monkey can move either one square diagonally, or one step sideways (to the right or left). That is, it makes the same moves as a King, except for stepping straight forward or backward.

The Blink Monkey is a piece which appears in Dai dai shōgi (大大将棋) and some other large Shōgi variants.

The Drunken Ferz, the Diabolo, and the Blind Bear (from Taikyoku shōgi) move in the same way.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Blind Monkey visits all the squares, filling the squares in roughly a flattened hexagon.

Wedge

Folded

Since the Blind Monkey can move as the Flying Cock, it can follow the numbers and fill the complete board trivially.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Blind Monkey fills the board row by row, alternating going with and against the numbers, with a small twist just before reaching the left hand side of the Wedge. It's visits the squares in the same order the King does.
