%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% combines the moves from the Queen and the Knight, which makes it a Knighted Piece. The %%PIECE%% can either slide an unlimited amount of squares in one the eight main directions, or jump to a square which is two squares away in one orthogonal direction, and one square away in the other.

This piece has a long history, and appears in many chess variants. As such, it's known under various names, including:

Spiral

Square

On the Spiral, since the %%PIECE%% can move as the Queen, it can move like the Wazir, and hence will follow the spiral, visiting all the squares on the board.

Diamond

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Blind Monkey, the %%PIECE%% will fille the entire board by following the Diamond Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, since %%PIECE%% can move as the Queen, it can also move as the Flying Cock. Hence it will visit all the squares on the board, in order of the values.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% fills the entire board, in a rather simple pattern.
