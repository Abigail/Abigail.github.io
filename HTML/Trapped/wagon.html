Wagon

by Abigail

The Wagon slides an unlimited amout of unoccupied squares in any orthogonal direction, but it may not land on a square next to the starting square (but the square must be unoccupied if the Wagon wants to move in that direction).

The Wagon in Scirocco, and Typhoonpromotes to the Spider.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Wagon moves in the same way as the Dabbaba, visiting only those squares which are both on even rows and even files. This follows the Spiral, blown up by a factor of two, visiting 25% of the board.

Wedge

On both the Folded Wedge and Flat Wedge, on each even row the Wagon visits the third square from the right, and the right most square, before jumping up two rows, escaping to infinity.
