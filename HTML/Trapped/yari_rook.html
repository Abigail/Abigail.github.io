The Yari Rook moves like a Rook, except backwards. That is, it may slide an unlimited number of squares sideways or forward.

Game Piece Info
Yari Shōgi Yari Rook ☊Promotes to Rook

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Yari Rook escapes to the forward right, by visiting four squares on each row forward from the origin. A five step loop moves the piece one square forward and to the right, resulting in an escape velocity of \(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{5}\).

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Yari Rook steps one square to the right on each step, escaping in the straight line with an escape velocity of \(1\).

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Yari Rook visits about half the squares on each row, alternating the right most and left most half of each row.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Yari Rook escapes to the upper right, using a step wise pattern to visit the two right most squares on each row. The escape velocity is \(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\).
