Marquis

by Abigail

The Marquis is a combined leaper, combining the moves of the Wazir and Knight. That is, the Marquis either moves one square in an orthogonal direction, or jumps to a square which is two squares away in one orthogonal direction, and one away in the other.

In Scirocco, the Marquis promotes to the Abbot.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Marquis can move as the Wazir. As such, it will follow the Spiral, filling the board in a boring way.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Marquis fills the board going from one side to another, bouncing up and down between two successive rows.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Marquis also fills the board going from one side to another, bouncing up and down between two succesive rows, but it uses a slightly different pattern as on the Folded Wedge.
