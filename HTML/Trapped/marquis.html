The Marquis is a Compound Leaper, combining the moves of the Wazir and Knight. That is, the %%PIECE%% either moves one square in any orthogonal direction, or jumps to a square which is two squares away in one orthogonal direction, and one away in the other.

Game Piece Info
Scirocco; Typhoon Marquis ☊Promotes to Abbot

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Marquis can move as the Wazir. As such, it will follow the Spiral, filling the board in a boring way.

Diamond

It's not clear whether the Marquis gets trapped on the Diamond Spiral. It does not get trapped in its first billion steps.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Marquis fills the board going from one side to another, bouncing up and down between two successive rows.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Marquis also fills the board going from one side to another, bouncing up and down between two succesive rows, but it uses a slightly different pattern as on the Folded Wedge.
