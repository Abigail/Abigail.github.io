Flying Cock

by Abigail
. . . . . . * . * . . * S * . . . . . . Shogi: 1

The Flying Cock either steps one square sideways, or one square diagonally forward.

Name Game(s) Info
Flying Cock Taikyoku Shōgi (大局将棋); Wa Shōgi (和将棋)

The piece is also known as the Sidewinder.

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Flying Cock escapes to infinity on a 45° angle, visiting three squares on each row, before moving one row up and one column back. This four step loop to move one square to the upper right means an escape velocity of \(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4}\).

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Flying Cock uses a two step loop to move one square upwards. This gives in an escape velocity of \(\frac{1}{2}\).

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Flying Cock has exactly the minimum movements available to follows the numbers in exact order.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Flying Cock visits almost every square of the board, filling the board row by row, to only skip the two left-most squares on every second row.
