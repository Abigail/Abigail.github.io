The Burning Chariot either slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares diagonally forward (like a forward Bishop), slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares orthogonally forward or backward (like a vertical Rook), or steps one square sideways (like a horizontal Wazir).

Name Game(s) Info
Burning Chariot Taikyoku Shōgi

Spiral

Square

The Burning Chariot moves like the Wazir, and visits all the squares on the board.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Burning Chariot uses a two step loop to move one square forward, giving in an escape velocity of \(\frac{1}{2}\).

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Burning Chariot moves like the Flying Cock, visiting all the squares on the board.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Burning Chariot visits all the squares on the board, using the same path the King uses.
