Dragon Horse

by Abigail

The Dragon Horse combines the moves of the King and the Bishop. That is, it either moves one square orthogonally, or slides an unlimited number of unoccupied squares diagonally.

In Shōgi, the Dragon Horse is the promoted Bishop.

The Scirocco moves in the same way as the Dragon Horse. In Scirocco, the Scirocco promotes to the Vulture. In Typhoon, the Alfil promotes to the Scirocco.

Spiral

The Dragon Horse can move as the Wazir, and hence just follow the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

The Dragon Horse can move as the Flying Cock, and hence just follow the Folded Wedge.

Flat

Just like the King, on the Flat Wedge, the Dragon Horse just goes left to right, and right to left, with a little sidestep before reaching the left hand side, filling the entire board.
