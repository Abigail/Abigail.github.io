%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% combines the moves of the Wazir and the Bishop. That is, it either moves one square orthogonally, or slides an unlimited number of unoccupied squares diagonally.

Spiral

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Wazir, and hence just follow the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Flying Cock, and hence just follow the Folded Wedge.

Flat

Just like the King, on the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% just goes left to right, and right to left, with a little sidestep before reaching the left hand side, filling the entire board.
