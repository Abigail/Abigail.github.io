Wizard

by Abigail

The Wizard moves as either the Ferz, or the Camel. This makes the Wizard a colour-bound piece. The piece is found in Omega Chess.

It is unclear whether the Wizard gets trapped on the Spiral.

On the Wedge, the Wizard follows a pretty pattern, visiting 50% of the squares.
