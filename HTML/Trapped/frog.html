Frog

by Abigail

The Frog is a piece which is used in Fairy Chess problems. It's an amphibian.

The Frog either leaps three squares in any of the four orthogonal directions, or steps one square diagonally.

A piece with the same name also appeared in the original version of Scirocco Chess, but it has disappered from the revised version. This piece moves differently though.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Frog doesn't get trapped for the first 20,000 steps, but the path it traces doesn't seem to be regular enough to state it will never get trapped.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Frog will visit all the squares.

Flate

On the Flat Wedge, the Frog will also visit all the squares, having a slight bias to the right hand side of the Flat Wedge.
