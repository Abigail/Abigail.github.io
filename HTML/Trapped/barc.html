The Barc leaps to a square which is either two squares away sideways (right or left) and one square forward, or to a square which is one away sideways and two squares backward. This half the moves a Knight has, and it mirrors the movements of the Crab.

Game Piece Info
Barc

Link: Cⱽ

Spiral

Square

After visiting mostly squares below the origin, the Barc gets trapped after 154 steps, which is less than half the steps the Crab uses.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, it takes the Barc just over twice the amount of steps to get trapped compared to the Square Spiral. Bouncing around in a roughly triangle shaped area, the Barc gets trapped after 325 steps.

Wedge

On neither the Folded, nor the Flat Wedge, the Barc can move.
