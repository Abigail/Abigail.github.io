The Nightrider moves one or more squares like a Knight, where each subsequents moves must be in the same direction as the first, and each square may not be occupied.

Spiral

Square

Surpringly, despite the Nightrider having more squares to move to in each steps, the Nightrider gets trapped sooner than the Knight. Where the Knight gets trapped in 2,015 steps, the Nightrider gets trapped after 509 steps!

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Nightrider gets trapped after 1,687 steps. This is less than half the number of steps the Knight makes before getting trapped (3,722).

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Nightrider gets trapped after 60 steps, which is 10 steps more than the Knight.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Nightrider gets trapped after 22 steps, while the Knight escapes.
