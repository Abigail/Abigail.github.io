%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves one or more squares like a Knight, where each subsequents moves must be in the same direction as the first, and each square may not be occupied.

Spiral

Surpringly, despite the %%PIECE%% having more squares to move to in each steps, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped sooner than the Knight. Where the Knight gets trapped in 2,015 steps, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped after 509 steps!

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped after 60 steps, which is 10 steps more than the Knight.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped after 22 steps, while the Knight escapes.
