%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves as either the King or the Knight. That is, it either moves to a square one away orthogonally or diagonally, or to a square which is two away in one orthogonal direction and one in the other.

There is also the Cavalier King, which moves the same way as the %%PIECE%%, except that its Knight move may be blocked.

Square

The %%PIECE%% can move like the Wazir, so it will just follow the Spiral and visit all the squares on the board.

Diamond

The %%PIECE%% can move like the Blind Monkey, so it will just follow the Spiral and visit all the squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% can move like the Flying Cock, so it will follow the Folded Wedge and visit all the squares.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% moves in a simple pattern, visiting all the squares on the board, with a bias to the right hand side of the board.
