Caliph

by Abigail

The Caliph moves as either the Wazir or the Alfil. As such, it's a compound leaper, and is used by Problemists.

Overkill Ecumenical Chess has a Bishop + Camel compound with the same name.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Caliph moves like the Wazir, following the Spiral, visiting all the squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Caliph visits all the squares, using an interesting pattern. The pattern is remarkable in the sense it has a bias to the left side of the wedge. Most filling pieces either do not have a bias, or have one on the right hand side of the Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Caliph follows a step wise pattern on the right edge of the Wedge, escaping to infinity, visiting only the two right most squares of each row.
