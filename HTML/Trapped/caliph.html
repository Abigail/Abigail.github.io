%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either moves one square orthogonally (like the Wazir) or jumps to a square which is two away diagonally (like the Alfil). As such, it's a compound leaper.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the %%PIECE%% moves like the Wazir, following the Spiral, visiting all the squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% visits all the squares, using an interesting pattern. The pattern is remarkable in the sense it has a bias to the left side of the wedge. Most filling pieces either do not have a bias, or have one on the right hand side of the Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% follows a step wise pattern on the right edge of the Wedge, escaping to infinity, visiting only the two right most squares of each row.
