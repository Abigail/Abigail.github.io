%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves like a Camelrider, except that it alternates between two different Camel moves on each step. It will always move outwards, and every second move is either on diagonally three squares away, or orthogonally four squares away. The %%PIECE%% is colourboundA colourbound piece will always stay on a square of the same colour: if it starts on a black square, it will always remain on a black square; if it starts on a white square, it will always remain on a white square.
