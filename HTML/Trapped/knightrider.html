Kightrider

by Abigail

A Knightrider moves one or more squares like a Knight, where each subsequents moves must be in the same direction as the first, and each square may not be occupied.

Spiral

Surpringly, despite the Knightrider having more squares to move to in each steps, the Knightrider gets trapped sooner than the Knight. Where the Knight gets trapped in 2015 steps, the Knightrider gets trapped after 509 steps!

Wedge

On the Wedge, the Knightrider gets trapped after 60 steps, which is 10 steps more than the Knight.
