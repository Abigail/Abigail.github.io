Right Iron Chariot

by Abigail
. . . . . . . . . . . * . . . . . * S * . . . . * * . . . . * . . . . . * . . . . . A . . . . . . Shogi: 1

The Right Iron Chariot either steps one square in any of the four orthogonal directions (like a Wazir), or slides an unlimited number of unoccupied squares diagonally backward to the left (like a left backward Bishop).

This mirrors the movement of the Left Iron Chariot.

Name Game(s) Info
Right Iron Chariot Taikyoku Shōgi

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Right Iron Chariot moves like the Wazir, visiting all the squares on the board.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Right Iron Chariot uses a simple two step loop to move one square backward. This way, it escapes with an escape velocity of \(\frac{1}{2}\).

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Right Iron Chariot get trapped after 6 steps, in the same way the Wazir does.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Right Iron Chariot visits the two right most squares of each row: a two step loop to move one square diagonally forward. This gives it an escape velocity of \(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\).
