Eagle (鵰)

by Abigail

The Eagle either slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares diagonally forward, slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied unoccupied squares orthogonally backwards, steps one square in each of eight main directions, or slides two squares diagonally backwards (where the square in between must be unoccupied).

The Eagle is a piece from Tori Shōgi. It's not present on the board at the start of the game — it appears when a Falcon promotes.

Spiral

Since the Eagle can move like the Wazir, the piece will do so, following the Spiral and visiting all the squares in order.

Wedge

Folded

Since the Eagle can move as the Flying Cock, the Goose will follow the numbers.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Goose fills the board row by row, alternating going with and against the numbers, with a small twist just before reaching the left hand side of the Wedge. This is the same pattern the Phoenix uses.
