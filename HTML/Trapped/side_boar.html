Side Boar

by Abigail
. . . . . . . . . . . . * * * . . . A * * * S * * * A . . . * * * . . . . . . . . . . . .

The Side Boar combines the movements of the King and the horizontal Rook. That is, it either steps one square in any of the eight cardinal directions, or slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares to the left or right.

Compare with the Flying Stag which moves the same way, but rotated by 90°.

Name Game(s) Info
Side Boar Taikyoku Shōgi

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Side Boar moves as the Wazir, and hence, will follow the Spiral, visiting all the squares.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Side Boar moves as the Blind Monkey, and hence, will visit all the squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

On the Diamond Spiral, the Side Boar moves as the Flying Cock, and hence, will visit all the squares on the board.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Side Boar visits all the squares on the board, with a strong bias to the upper right.
