Crane (鶴)

by Abigail

The Crane either steps one square forward, either diagonally, or straight ahead, or one square backwards, either diagonally or straight backwards.

The Crane is a piece from Tori Shōgi.

The Ferocious Leopard from Chu Shōgi moves in the same way.

Spiral

The Crane visits all the squares on the Spiral. The squares visited form an expanding hexagon, which is kind of unusual.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Crane visits the right most three squares of each row, escaping to infinity.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Crane only visits the right most square of each row, escaping to infinity quickly.
