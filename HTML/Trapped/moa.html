%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves as the Knight, but it may not jump. It moves one square diagonally (which must be unoccupied), then one square orthogonally outward.

Compare this to the Mao which reaches the same squares, but first moves orthogonally, then diagonally.

Game Piece Info
Problemists Moa

Link: Cⱽ

Spiral

Square

The %%PIECE%% gets trapped quickly, after no more than 21 steps.

Diamond

The %%PIECE%% gets trapped quickly, after 35 steps.

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% uses the two step loop to move one square forward and to the right, for an escape velocity of \(\sqrt{2}\).

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% moves exactly as it does on the Folded Wedge. So, it escapes with an escape velocity of \(\sqrt{2}\).
