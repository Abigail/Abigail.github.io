Root 50 Leaper

by Abigail

A Root 50 Leaper combines the movements of the (5,5)-leaper and the (7,1)-leaper. The distance the piece travels in each move is \(\sqrt{50}\), hence its name.

Neither on the Spiral, nor on the Wedge, does the Root 50 Leaper get trapped in the first 20,000 steps.
