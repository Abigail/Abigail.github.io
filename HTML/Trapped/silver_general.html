%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either moves one square diagonally, or one square orthogonally forward. It appears in several games, under different names.

The Humpback mirrors the movements of the %%PIECE%%.

Spiral

The %%PIECE%% visits all the squares of the Spiral, in a unique pattern, with a strong bias to the upper right.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% follows a zig-zag pattern on the right of the Wedge, escaping to infinity.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% visits the right most square of each row, and not other rows.
