Silver General

by Abigail

The Silver General either moves one square diagonally, or one square orthogonally forward.

The Silver General (銀將) is found in Shōgi (将棋). When it promotes, it moves like a Gold General.

Spiral

The Silver General visits all the squares of the Spiral, in a unique pattern, with a strong bias to the upper right.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Silver General follows a zig-zag pattern on the right of the Wedge, escaping to infinity.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Silver General visits the right most square of each row, and not other rows.
