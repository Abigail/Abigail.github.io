Knight (Shōgi)

by Abigail

The Knight jumps to a square which is two squares ahead, and one square sideways.

The Knight is a piece in Shōgi (将棋), and promotes to the Gold General.

This is a different piece than the Knight found in (Western) Chess and its variants.

Spiral

The Knight escapes in a straight line to infinity.

Wedge

On both the Folded and the Flat Wedge, the Knight escapes to infinity using the same line as the Spiral.
