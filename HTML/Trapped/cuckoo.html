Cuckoo

by Abigail
. . . . . . . * . . . . . . . . * S * . . . . . .

The Cuckoo either moves one square sideways (like a sideways Wazir), or two squares orthogonally forward (like a forward Dabbaba).

Game Piece Info
Typhoon Cuckoo ☊Promotes to Longleaper; 🎩This piece has additional abilities,
which we can ignore here

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the Cuckoo visits every second row going forward from the origin. It will visits that part where the numbers on the Spiral are consecutive (so, from where the spiral turns left on to the row, till where it the spiral turns left again). As such, it will visit 12.5% of the board.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the Cuckoo moves in a straight line to the right, one square at the time, for an escape velocity of 1.

Wedge

On both the Folded Wedge and the Flat Wedge, the Cuckoo will visit the three right most squares of every second row, escaping to infinity that way. It's a three step loop to move to a square which is two away to the upper left. This results in an escape velocity of \(\frac{2 \sqrt{2}}{3}\).
