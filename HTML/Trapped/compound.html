Compound Pieces

by Abigail

Compound pieces combine the movement of two traditional Chess pieces. The Queen in Chess is a example of a compound piece, combining the Bishop and Rook.

We can classify the compound pieces into two classes: the Knighted pieces which combine a piece with a Knight, and the Crowned pieces, which combine a piece with a King.
