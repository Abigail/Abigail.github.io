Horned Owl

by Abigail

The Horned Owl either moves up to four unoccupied squares diagonally (like a restricted Bishop), jumps to a square two away in a orthogonal direction, or one square orthogonally forward. Additionally, the Horned Owl has some hopping capture moves and some non-moving captures available, as well as the option to pass. But we can ignore those.

In Typhoon, the Gold General promotes to the Horned Owl.

Spiral

On the Spiral, the Horned Owl doesn't get trapped for its first 20,000 steps. It doesn't quite settle into a repeating pattern, so while it looks like it doesn't get trapped, we don't know for sure.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the Horned Owl visits very odd square of each row, so in the limit, it will visit half of the squares on the board.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Horned Owl uses an interesting pattern making multiple sweeps over each row to visit all the squares.
