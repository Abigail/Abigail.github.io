%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% first moves one square orthogonally (like the Wazir), then one or more squares diagonally outwards (like the Bishop). It cannot end its move next to the square it started on (see the Spider for the piece which can).

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped quickly, after no more than 14 steps.

Flat

The %%PIECE%% gets trapped on the Flat Wedge even quicker, after 12 steps.
