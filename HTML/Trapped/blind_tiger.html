Blind Tiger

by Abigail
. . . . . . * . * . . * S * . . * * * . . . . . . Shogi: 1

The Blind Tiger moves one step in all directions (diagonally and orthogonally), except straight forward. It is the mirror image of the Drunk Elephant which moves in all directions, except straight backwards.

Name Game(s) Info
Blind Tiger Chu Shōgi; Taikyoku Shōgi; Typhoon

Spiral

Square

The Blind Tiger fills the entire board, but it does so in an unusual manner. It fills up the board in an expanding, roughly triangle shaped pattern. It adds three rows at the bottom, for each row at the right and left side of the triangle. This is the mirror of the travel pattern of the Drunk Elephant.

Diamond

The Blind Tiger can move as the Blind Monkey. Hence, it will follow the Diamond Spiral, and visit all the squares on the board.

Wedge

Folded

The Blind Tiger can move as the Flying Cock, causing the Blind Tiger to follow the numbers.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the Blind Tiger moves in the same way as the King does, filling the wedge row by row, going from right to left and left to right, with a small side step before reaching the left end of each row.
