%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves one square in any direction, except backwards. This is a mirror of the movement of the Blind Tiger which moves one square in any direction except forwards.

Spiral

The %%PIECE%% fills the entire board, but it does so in an unusual manner. It fills up the board in an expanding, roughly triangle shaped pattern. It adds three rows at the top, for each row at the right and left side of the triangle.

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Flying Cock, causing the %%PIECE%% to follow the numbers.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% moves in the same way as the King does, filling the wedge row by row, going from right to left and left to right, with a small side step before reaching the left end of each row.
