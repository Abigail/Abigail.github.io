Deva

by Abigail
. . . . . . * * . . . * S * . . * * * . . . . . . Shogi: 1

The Deva moves one square in any direction, except diagonally right forward.

The Deva moves mirror the moves of the Dark Spirit.

Name Game(s) Info
Deva Taikyoku Shōgi

Spiral

Square

The Deva can move like the Wazir, hence it visits every square by just following the Spiral.

Diamond

The Deva visits every square on the board. The path it follows never intersects itself, and alternates between going clockwise and counter clockwise.

Wedge

Folded

The Deva visits every square on the board following a path which never intersects itself.

Flat

The Deva escapes with a velocity of \(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\). The two right most squares of every row are visited.
