%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% is a (4,1)-Leaper. That is, from its starting square, it jumps to a square which is four away in one orthogonal direction, and one in the other.

Game Piece Info
Leapers Giraffe Do not confuse this with the Giraffe from Dōbutsu shōgi or the Giraffe from Turkish Great Chess and Eric's Great Chess

Spiral

Square

The %%PIECE%% moves irregular on the Square Spiral, eventually getting trapped. This, however, only happens on step 13,102.

Diamond

The %%PIECE%% gets trapped faster on the Diamond Square; after 1.322 steps, it is trapped.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped much faster than on either Spiral: after a mere 114 steps, it gets trapped.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% gets trapped even faster; it makes 94 steps, and gets trapped.
