%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either moves one square diagonally (like a Ferz), one square right or left (like a sideways Wazir), or an unlimited number of unoccupied squares orthogonally forward or backward (like a vertical Rook).

Game Piece Info
Chu Shōgi (中将棋) Flying Stag 飛鹿 ☋Is promoted Blind Tiger

Spiral

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Wazir, and hence, will visit all the squares by following the Spiral.

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% can move as the Flying Cock, and hence, will visit all the squares by following the Folded Wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% will visit all the squares, using the same pattern the King uses.
