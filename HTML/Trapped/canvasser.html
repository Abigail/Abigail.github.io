%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% either slides an unlimited amount of unoccupied squares in an orthogonal direction (like the Rook), or jumps to a square which is three away in one orthogonal direction, and one away in the other (it jumps like the Camel).

Spiral

Square

Since the %%PIECE%% can move as the Rook, it can also move as the Wazir, which means it can follow the Spiral, filling the entire board in a boring pattern.

Diamond

It's not clear whether the %%PIECE%% gets trapped on the Diamond Spiral. It does not get trapped for its first billion moves.

Wedge

Folded

On the Folded Wedge, the %%PIECE%% visits all the squares on the board, filling the Wedge going left to right, and right to back, using a handful of repeating patterns.

Flat

The %%PIECE%% visits all the square of the Flat Wedge, using a repeating pattern, and filling the board with a bias to the right hand side.
