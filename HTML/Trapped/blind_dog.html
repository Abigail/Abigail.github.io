%%PIECE%%

by Abigail

The %%PIECE%% moves to a square which orthogonally right, left or backwards one away (like a backward/sideways Wazir), or to a square which is one away diagonally forward (like a forward Ferz).

Game Piece Info
Wa Shōgi (和将棋) Blind Dog 盲犬 ☊Promotes to Violent Wolf

Spiral

Square

On the Square Spiral, the %%PIECE%% fills the entire board, in a unique pattern.

Diamond

On the Diamond Spiral, the %%PIECE%% escapes upward, using the two step loop to move to the square above it started the loop from. This means its escape velocity is \(\frac{1}{2}\).

Wedge

Folded

The %%PIECE%% can moves as the Flying Cock, hence, it will follow the wedge.

Flat

On the Flat Wedge, the %%PIECE%% will visit every square, mostly filling rows right to left and left to right, with a small side step just before reaching the left end of a row.
