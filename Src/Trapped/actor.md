On the Diamond Spiral, it's not clear whether the **Actor** will get
trapped or not. It won't get trapped for its first three billion steps.

Almost half the moves of the **Actor** on the Diamond Spiral are
one-square %%BISHOP%% moves (aka %%FERZ%% moves). Overall, beside
the one-square %%BISHOP%% moves, the **Actor** mostly makes %%CAMEL%%
and %%KNIGHT%% moves. But it does make longer and longer %%BISHOP%%
moves; the longest is 40,919 squares in the first three billion steps.

an interesting pattern. It fills the Wedge in a balanced way.

Folded Wedge, it uses only 6 of its possible 8 %%CAMEL%% moves. And except
for one three-square %%BISHOP%% moves, all the %%BISHOP%% moves it
makes are one-square moves, aka %%FERZ%% moves.
