The **Hawk** either moves as the [*Alfil*](alfil.html),
the [*Dabbaba*](dabbaba.html), the [*Threeleaper*](threeleaper.html)
or the [*Tripper*](tripper.html).
That it, it steps two or three squares in each of the eight main
directions.
